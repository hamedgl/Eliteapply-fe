# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students and applicants managing university programmes, scholarships, fellowships and grants across countries and high-stakes deadlines.

## Product Purpose

EliteApply is a calm operating system for academic applications, documents, writing, references and interviews.

## Brand Personality

Academically credible, calm and focused. Premium without exclusivity.

## Anti-references

Avoid career terminology, generic AI styling, fake metrics, admission guarantees and misleading unfinished capabilities.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next responsible action obvious.
2. Use honest backend state.
3. Keep high-stakes workflows calm and recoverable.
4. Preserve student control over sensitive evidence.
5. Prefer familiar accessible patterns over novelty.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA, keyboard operation, visible focus, semantic labels, reduced motion, non-color cues and touch-friendly responsive controls.
